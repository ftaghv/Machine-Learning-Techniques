Course Logistics and Policies

Prerequisites Linear Algebra, Probability & Statistics, Calculus and Python

Homeworks Students will complete one homework assignment after each class session, for a total of 9 assignments. Each homework will focus on the concepts covered that day and will include both theoretical questions and hands-on coding exercises.
